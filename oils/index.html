Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I start?

First, you will need to determine if you would like to be a Wellness Advocate or a Wholesale Customer. You will find the corresponding button on the top of this page. Click the button of your choice to learn more.

How much does it cost to join?

The cost for just an annual dōTERRA membership is $35. If you start with an enrollment kit, that waives the membership fee and includes the most popular essential oil products. Enrollment kits start at $150.

Why should I join through Oils Network?

You will get support and amazing team members. Join our over 350,000 members! Join a team where your upline cares about you.

Do I need to get a kit to sign up?

You do not need to purchase a kit to join doTERRA. For $35 you will become a member for a whole year.

As a wholesale customer, do I need to sell oils?

As a customer you will not be expected to sell oils. That is 100% optional. 

What is the loyalty rewards program?

The loyalty rewards program, also called LRP, allows members who order monthly to earn free product points. These can be used for cash purchases.

How do I get commissions?

doTERRA is structured with unilevel enrollment. Unlike other compensation plans, doTERRA increase the percentage at the lower levels. This allows for greater payout potential. There are weekly payouts, monthly payouts, as well as bonuses.

As a wholesale member, do I have to buy products every month?

No. A wholesale member does not need to purchase every month. You can purchase whenever you want.
